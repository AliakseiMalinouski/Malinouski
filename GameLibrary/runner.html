JoRun

JoRun — 2d ranger style game, The story is about a travelling archaeologist named Joe, who, through his own bad luck, though perhaps fortunately for him, is an archaeologist, has stumbled into a dinosaur's lair. Now it's up to Joe to escape the dinosaur, or be eaten. Rules are easy: once "Space" "Touch" - Joe is taking the leap. Good luck!
